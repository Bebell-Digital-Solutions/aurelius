5. YOUR RIGHTS OVER YOUR DATA

The Grove belongs to you, Strategist. You have full sovereignty over your data.

5.1 Under the GDPR (EU/EEA/UK)

  • Right to Access – request a copy of all data we hold.
  • Right to Rectification – correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to Erasure – request deletion ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to Restrict Processing – limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Data Portability – receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object – object to processing based on legitimate interests.

5.2 Under the CCPA/CPRA (California)

  • Right to Know – request details on data collected, used, and shared.
  • Right to Delete – request deletion of personal information.
  • Right to Opt-Out – opt-out of sale or sharing (we do not sell data).
  • Right to Non-Discrimination – no penalty for exercising rights.

5.3 How to Exercise Your Rights

  • Email: privacy@aurelius.ai
  • Subject line: Privacy Request: [Your Request]
  • Verification: We may request proof of identity.

We respond within 30 days (GDPR) or 45 days (CCPA) of verification.

6. DATA SECURITY

Despite these measures, no system is 100% secure. We will notify you promptly of any breach affecting your personal data.
