📜 Default Prompts Library

Timing

"Sun Tzu said: 'The quality of decision is like the well-timed swoop of a falcon.'"

📜 Principle: Speed kills—but haste is suicide.

💭 Reflection: What situation are you rushing into right now that actually requires patience?

⚔️ Action: Delay your biggest decision for 24 hours. Use that time to gather two pieces of contrary evidence.

Terrain

"Clausewitz wrote: 'War is the realm of friction.'"

📜 Principle: The 'fog of war' always obscures the truth.

💭 Reflection: What hidden 'friction' is slowing your current project?

⚔️ Action: Identify the single biggest source of friction today and eliminate it with one phone call.

Positioning

"Machiavelli taught: 'The wise ruler never shows his hand.'"

📜 Principle: Visibility invites attack.

💭 Reflection: Are you revealing your strategy too early to people who do not need to know it?

⚔️ Action: Identify one piece of information you shared recently that you could have kept private. Correct that tomorrow.

Adaptation

"Darwin observed: 'It is not the strongest that survive, but those most responsive to change.'"

📜 Principle: Rigidity breaks. Adaptability endures.

💭 Reflection: What belief about your industry or role are you clinging to that is becoming outdated?

⚔️ Action: Spend 15 minutes reading something that challenges your current worldview.

Decisiveness

"Musashi wrote: 'The way of the warrior is the resolute acceptance of death.'"

📜 Principle: Indecision is a decision to fail.

💭 Reflection: What decision have you been avoiding because you fear making the wrong choice?

⚔️ Action: Make that decision today. If uncertain, choose the path that preserves maximum optionality.

📜 Principle: Pride makes you predictable.

⚔️ Action: In your next conversation, let someone else take the credit for your idea.

⚔️ Action: Do one small, unexpected favor for someone who matters to your goals.

📜 Principle: Reality is framed, not objective.
